Our Backyard

Relax the Kiwi way in Our Backyard! Reveal Lucky Numbers in the caravans and match them to kiwis in Your Numbers to win a prize. Keep an eye out for the Cash symbol – revealing them awards an instant prize! Time to chill out and soak up the sun!

How to Bet

Ticket Cost

Displays the current ticket cost.

Press the left arrow (–) to decrease the ticket cost.

Press the right arrow (+) to increase the ticket cost.

Press BUY to deduct the ticket cost from the balance and play the game.

How to Play

Select symbols to reveal numbers. Match any Your Number to any Winning Number to win a prize. Reveal a Cash symbol to win a prize instantly.

After all numbers are revealed, a closing message appears, indicating the game’s outcome.

Auto Reveal

Press AUTO REVEAL to have the game play to completion automatically. Game play continues until all selections have been made.

Rules

Misuse or malfunction voids all pays and plays.

Instant wins are added to total winning prize values.

Wins display as currency.

Player choices made during gameplay do not affect the game’s outcome.
